Product Information

Attach your Moose Knuckle Offroad shackle or tow shackle with ease using the Mohawk 1.25, a versatile SxS, ATV, and UTV shackle hitch receiver.

Precision warm forged from 6082 aluminum, the Mohawk 1.25 is designed with optimized grain flow for maximum strength, delivering a 7,000 lb workload limit.

Dual hitch pin holes provide two mounting positions, ensuring compatibility with any 1.25-inch hitch mounting tube. A third hitch pin hole and included spacer allow for 90-degree rotation, accommodating a hitch ball with a 3/4" shank for 300 lbs tongue weight and 3,500 lbs gross trailer weight, making it Class I/II hitch ball compatible.

Unlike traditional shackle receivers with sharp edges that damage soft shackles, synthetic ropes, and strap loops, the Mohawk 1.25 features a large radius main mounting hole, reducing abrasion and extending the life of your recovery gear. Its spined outer perimeter reinforces critical areas while adding a bold, aggressive look.
